## What is Snippet AI ?

**Snippet AI** is an advanced content repurposing tool built for the event industry.

It automatically detects key moments from your video or audio sessions, extracts the most engaging clips, and generates ready-to-share content such as:

- Short videos
- Key takeaways
- Speaker highlights
- Auto-generated summaries
- Social-ready visuals and thumbnails

With Snippet AI, you can give your event content a **second life** — perfect for social media, newsletters, microsites, and post-event engagement.
